About Us

Mr. Budhi chand Gupta

Since its inception in 1960, TDNFL, founded by the visionary Mr. Budhi Chand Gupta (Founder), has emerged as a paramount figure in the cotton industry, pioneering excellence in every facet of its operations. With an unwavering dedication to quality and customer satisfaction, TDNFL has solidified its position as a cornerstone of the cotton sector in India.

Our journey begins with the meticulous sourcing of raw cotton, followed by a streamlined process of production and distribution, culminating in the delivery of premium-grade bales to clients across the nation. Renowned for our uncompromising standards and steadfast commitment to excellence, we take immense pride in not only meeting but exceeding the expectations of our esteemed clientele.

Beyond our core cotton offerings, TDNFL ventures into premium cotton seed production, leveraging cutting-edge techniques to cultivate superior seeds. These seeds, in turn, serve as the foundation for our range of nutritious cattle feed and versatile cottonseed oil, further enriching our product portfolio and contributing to the agricultural ecosystem.

At TDNFL, innovation is at the heart of everything we do. We are relentless in our pursuit of enhancing product quality, exploring new technologies, and championing sustainable practices. Our endeavors extend beyond the confines of the industry as we actively support farmers and the agricultural sector, fostering growth and prosperity across communities in India.

As we forge ahead, guided by the principles of integrity, innovation, and excellence, TDNFL remains steadfast in its commitment to shaping the future of the cotton industry and empowering stakeholders every step of the way.
